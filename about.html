Zach Michel is a nineteen-year-old Graphic Design college student who started a little business called "ExZACHt Earrings" in late 2019. Zach has always been an avid flyfisherman, and the hobby gave him the opportunity to learn how to tie flies. Since he has a background in fly tying, he only had to buy some earring hooks and start tying feathers onto them to start making earrings. He had the idea of starting the business last August of 2019 when some of his friends said that they would buy a pair of his earrings. All of his advertisement is through either Instagram or Facebook, which allows anyone to be able to view his products without the cost of advertisement.

Zach has an aunt who currently resides in Atlanta, Georgia. She owns a clothing boutique and sells many different styles of clothes. When she saw Zach's earrings, she knew that they would sell very well in her boutique, so she bought many pairs. Zach has made quite the little business for himself, between online sales and this brick-and-mortar store. The money he earns from his business goes toward helping the young entrepreneur pay his way through college.
