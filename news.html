Fun facts about the company:
-Started on November 19, 2019
-Started with only one employee, now has 10
-Has one real shop, located in Atlanta
-Made $30,000 in profits the first three months of existence
-Bought lots of inventory to keep up with demand in December
-Hopes to capitalize on the recent influx of demand due to the increase of online shopping because of COVID-19

Company News
--Recently, due to the COVID-19 outbreak, employees have had a harder time managing inventory and shipping products. However, the company has decided that they will still ship out the usual amount of packages to their customers, all the while taking certain precautions so as to not spread the virus. Before the virus, our CEO and founder, Zach Michel, went to a flyfishing show in Lancaster P.A. This show displayed capes from different types of birds and provided him with a multitude of ideas for interesting earrings for the company. One last item of news: we are excited to announce that a stock investor, Thomas Shepherd, has purchased 10% of our company in exchange for $15,000! We are excited to bring him on board to our rising team. Welcome aboard, Mr. Shepherd!

Our hardworking employees are doing all they can to make sure you, the customers, can get your products on time and as advertised!
